Assessing Shoreline Changes in Funafuti Atoll, Tuvalu

Using Time Series Analysis of WorldView

Team Members:

Aaron Richmond-Crosset & Abbie Phillips

Affiliation:

GEOG 293: Intro to Remote Sensing

Project Description:

This project used Random Forest classification to assess shoreline change in Funafuti Atoll, Tuvalu. The analysis compared 2015 and 2024 imagery to identify areas of shoreline loss and gain. Results showed that smaller islands experienced the most erosion, while some eastern islands gained land through sediment deposition. These patterns highlight Funafuti’s increasing vulnerability to erosion and sea level rise.
